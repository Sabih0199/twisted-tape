CALGAVIN® is proud to say that we are recognised worldwide for our knowledge of the thermodynamics, heat transfer and fluid flow, a ‘one-stop shop’ for your heat transfer solutions using the latest software.
Our engineering team have grown their knowledge of heat transfer through the innovation and research of our heat transfer enhancement products.
Research is at the centre of CALGAVIN’s business, with a range of test facilities within its internal Research & Development department.
We also actively develop research programmes, in collaboration with United Kingdom and overseas universities, to support their cutting-edge research programmes.
New generation technology, tools and machines used to maintain high standard specifications.
We can manufacture a large variety of twisted tapes to meet any specification, considering even the tightest twist ratios.
A large variety of high-quality raw materials are stocked at CALGAVIN headquarters in Alcester, United Kingdom. Ready to meet your tight lead times.
Select from popular retaining methods or have them custom made to meet your specification requirements.
Our heat transfer enhancement products can be made from a variety of different materials. Any grade available in strip form (including Austenitic Steels) can be supplied.
Each order is custom made, expertly packed, and securely dispatched from our head office in Alcester, United Kingdom to your specified location anywhere around the world.
All our packaging is ISPM 15 compliant, heat treated and fumigated. Adding any distinctive shipping marking is possibly free of charge.
CALGAVIN®’s products can be installed in many different types of tubular heat exchangers and to many different tube dimensions. Our engineers provide the knowledge and service for an efficient install.
On-site or remote training can be provided.
